Welcome to the Conference

About the Conference:

International Conference on Recent Trends in Information, Communication and Electrical Engineering (ICRTICE 2024) provides an International platform for research scholars, academic scientists and students to share their expertise and contribute to innovative research and development in all aspects of Information Communication and Electrical Engineering. The conference also aims to bring people together from various fields but having the common interest and passion for research in further advancement in the field of intelligent systems, such an amalgamation of various research oriented minds will bear prolific results in future, which might lead to further advancements in such an era where anything is possible.

About the Institution:

Sethu Institute of Technology (SIT) is an Autonomous Institution established in 1995. It is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, accredited by NBA and NAAC with A++ grade. The college started with 180 students in the year 1995 with three UG programs has recorded a stupendous growth of expanding to the strength of 4800 students with 16 UG programs and 5 Post Graduate Programs. The College has been focus in Innovation and Research & Development as its goal since the inception of the College.
